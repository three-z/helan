Производство

CASELLA - GE

Our new headquarters are located in the area of Casella, situated in the green countryside behind Genoa, Italy. The building covers over 7,500 sq metres, surrounded by about 12,000 sq metres outside. The furnishings, beyond meeting our logistic and organizational needs, suit our ever-loyal philosophy linked to nature.

Each and every Helan product is realized within our laboratories, using craftsmanship and the help of technologically advanced equipment with impeccable plant and packaging hygiene.

Our new location opened up the opportunity to modernize the production plant, obtaining ample energy savings by using a steam system for equipment heating which then uses a closed cycle cooling system. This also consequentially reduces working water consumption.

The availability of these new facilities has furthermore allowed us to automate order processing in order to satisfy customer needs in the most timely manner.

MADE IN ITALY

Our products are entirely thought out, made and packaged in Italy to ensure proper care of all the company’s employees, impossible to verify for goods manufactured in other countries. We aim to avoid useless wastes in energy and transport for unfair quotes in order to do our small part in helping the country’s economy growth to offer our children a better future.
